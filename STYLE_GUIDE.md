## Typography
- **Primary Font**: `Kanit` (Sans-serif)
- **Fallback Fonts**: `ui-sans-serif`, `system-ui`, `sans-serif`
- **Usage Guidelines**:
  - **Headers**: Black (900) or Bold (700) weight, uppercase for hero/titles.
  - **Body**: Medium (500) or Regular (400) weight.
  - **Captions**: Light (300) weight, uppercase with tracking-widest.

## üöÄ Components & UI Patterns

### Buttons
- **Primary**: `bg-emerald-600 text-white`, rounded-2xl, shadow-lg.
- **Secondary**: `bg-white/10 backdrop-blur-xl text-white`, border-white/20.
- **Hover States**: Increase brightness or change background color slightly.

### Cards
- **Background**: `bg-white` or `bg-slate-50`.
- **Shadow**: `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md` for interactive elements.
- **Border**: `border-slate-100` or `border-slate-200`.

## ‚ôø Accessibility Standards
- **Contrast**: Ensure all text has a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 (WCAG AA).
- **Emerald on White**: `emerald-600` on white is compliant.
- **White on Emerald**: White text on `emerald-500/600` is compliant.
- **Status Indicators**: Use both color and icons for state changes (e.g., active/inactive).
